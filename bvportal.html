BV Portal

BV Portal will deliver a user-friendly and scalable platform that empowers students to take control of their attendance monitoring and leave management activities

The purpose of BV Portal is to develop a portal for Banasthali university to create a centralized platform for student, teacher and admin focusing on 2 key functionalities.
1. Teacher can take attendance directly on the application eliminating the need to manually fill the OMR sheet.
2. Student can access her attendance record anytime she can check if teacher has marked absent/present.
3.Student can also check the number of leaves she can take.
4.Admin can perform a no. of tasks: -Adding a new student in a class -Managing the schedule of a teacher -Granting leave to students
